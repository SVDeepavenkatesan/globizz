The Department of International Business, Pondicherry University, is organizing a Tree Plantation initiative as part of GLOBIZZ’26 to promote environmental sustainability and ecological responsibility. This initiative encourages students and participants to contribute towards a greener future by planting trees and supporting global environmental goals such as climate action and life on land. Join us in making a positive impact on the environment and fostering a culture of sustainability.
