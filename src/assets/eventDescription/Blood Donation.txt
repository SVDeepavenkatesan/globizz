The Department of International Business, Pondicherry University, in collaboration with the Lions Club of Thirukkanur, is organizing a Voluntary Blood Donation Drive as part of GLOBIZZ’26. This initiative aims to encourage individuals to donate blood and help save lives. By participating in this noble cause, donors contribute to supporting patients in need and strengthening community healthcare support.
